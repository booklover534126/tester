To defeat Metal Man in Mega Man 2, follow these strategies!

Use the Metal Blade:

Metal Man's weapon, the Metal Blade, can be used to deal damage to Metal Man.

Quick Boomerang:

Metal Man's counter-attack involves tossing a metal blade at his target. Using the Quick Boomerang can help dodge this attack.

Avoid Direct Contact:

Metal Man's attacks can be countered by jumping straight p into the air and tossing a metal blade at his target immediately after taking fire.

Stay on the Right:

When fighting Metal Man, try to stay on the right-most blocks to avoid his boomerang attacks.

Shoot from a Distance:

If Metal Man gets stuck in a corner, you can shoot him from a distance to win the fight.

By employing these strategies, you can effectively defeat

Metal Man and move on to the next challenge in Mega Man 2.
