To defeat Quick Man in Mega Man 2, follow these strategies!

Use Metal Blade:

Heat Man's Atomic Fire can be dodged by using the Metal Blade diagonally or through walls.

Avoid the molten lava:

Be cautious of the molten lava that can kill instantly.

Defeat the Tellies:

Use the Metal Blade to fire at Tellies that appear on the platforms.

Use the Atomic Fire:

Charge the Atomic Fire weapon to throw larger fireballs and deal significant damage.

Bubble Lead:

Use Bubble Lead to ignore the fire pillars and deal immense damage to Heat Man.

By employing these strategies, you can effectively defeat

Quick Man and move on to the next challenge in Mega Man 2.
