To defeat Wood Man in Mega Man 2, follow these strategies!

Defeat Battons:

Start by eliminating the Battons hanging from the trees, as they can be a significant threat. Use the Metal Blade to shoot them down before they attack.

Fight Frienders:

The Frienders are stationary but powerful. Use the Metal Blade to attack them when they breathe fire, and avoid their attacks by jumping to the right or left.

Climb and Jump:

Players should climb down the suspended platforms and jump across them to avoid attacks from Monkings and Pipis.

Avoid Cooks:

The Cooks are difficult to defeat, but players can avoid them by using the Metal Blade diagonally to shoot them down.

Final Boss Fight

The final challenge involves defeating the Cooks and the Boss. Use the Metal Blade to shoot them down and avoid their attacks.

By employing these strategies, you can effectively defeat

Wood Man and move on to the next challenge in Mega Man 2.
