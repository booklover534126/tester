To defeat Bubble Man in Mega Man 2, follow these strategies!

Avoid the bubble leads:

Bubble Man will shoot out Bubble Leads at you, which can be avoided by staying out of their range or using the Metal Blade to throw them diagonally..

Manage the Anko:

The Anko will spawn Shrimp robots from its mouth. Target the small lamp above its eyes to defeat it quickly.

Jump Carefully:

When Bubble Man swims upward, avoid jumping too high to prevent hitting the ceiling spikes

Use the Metal Blade:

The Metal Blade is particularly effective against the Shrimp robots and the Anko.

Stay Alert:

Keep an eye on the Bubble Leads and the Anko's movements to anticipate their attacks and avoid them.

By employing these strategies, you can effectively defeat

Bubble Man and move on to the next challenge in Mega Man 2.
